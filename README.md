## CONSO

A unified 5D Consumer Passport from your social, gaming, creative, and on-chain activity | Built during Haulout Hackathon

### Features

- **_5D Consumer Passport_** : Aggregate your digital identity across social, gaming, creative, and on-chain platforms into a single NFT passport.
- **_ZAPs Scoring System_** : Earn reputation points by connecting platforms and completing activities. Build your Consumer Percentile ranking.
- **_Zapboard Dashboard_** : A personalized control center that visualizes your connected accounts, badges, and ZAPs score across all zones.
